Welcome to NewLandlordHQ

NewLandlordHQ is the place to learn everything you need to be a successful landlord.  Every day people lose their homes to foreclosure or sell their homes for a loss.  But there is an alternative and it’s much easier than you may expect.

My name is John and there was a time that I considered selling my home for a loss (check out my full story here), but instead I became a landlord and still own that property today.  I’m here to teach you that you can do the same thing.
